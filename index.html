About Me


Dustin has a passion for the arts and holds a Master of Fine Arts in Ceramics from Louisiana Tech University and Bachelor of Fine Arts from Baylor University. He is a Co-Founder and current Managing Partner of DB McGilvray Ceramics, a boutique ceramic manufacturing company specializing in small production runs, customer orders, mold making and clay making. He pioneered the ground-up development of the company and successfully drafted, negotiated and finalized an exclusivity agreement that solidified a production sharing partnership with Mudshark Studios.
